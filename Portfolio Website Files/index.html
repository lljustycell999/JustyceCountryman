About Me

Welcome to my webpage! I'm currently completing my final semester at SUNY Oswego, majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics. Outside of my studies, I'm passionate about various interests, including spending quality time with my family, transforming my room into a dream man cave, and staying updated on severe weather patterns. I thrive on conquering academic challenges and excel in team environments, where I'm known for my leadership and problem-solving abilities.

One of my key approaches to tackling complex tasks involves a methodical cycle of understanding the problem, devising a strategic plan, implementing solutions, and iteratively refining until achieving optimal outcomes. This process, honed through numerous academic and real-world endeavors, underscores my commitment to delivering value in every endeavor.

I'm particularly excited about my current coursework in Software Engineering, where I'm involved in a team-based project. This experience not only tests my technical skills but also provides an opportunity to enhance my leadership abilities and foster a collaborative mindset among my peers.

I look forward to leveraging my diverse skill set and positive attitude to contribute meaningfully to future projects and initiatives.
